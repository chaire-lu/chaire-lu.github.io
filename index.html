This is a non-linear CTF

  • You will have multiple flags to capture
  • Flag notation is denoted via XXXX# {flag}
    • e.g. ACHR2: {flag} would signify the 2nd piece of the final answer, derived from the Acheron pathway.
  • Once all the flags are captured, they can be assembled numerically to form the final answer
  • There is no particular order you need to go in; you can start with any river !
  • Pick your poison, but not all chalices are present
